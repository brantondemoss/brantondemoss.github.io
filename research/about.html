I'm interested in the nature of emergence, which I study using algorithmic information theory and machine learning. A cell is alive, but only made of dead things. Water is wet, yet a single molecule is not. Emergence is the observation that systems exhibit new behavior at different degrees of scale and complexity.

Previously, I did research in mathematics and physics, with projects in knot theory, neutrino physics, and quantum many-body localization. In industry, I've worked at a few startups, writing code for fast geophysics simulations, and machine learning engineering at a few places, including a company I founded to help researchers accelerate literature reviews.
